Driving Tamil Nadu's Corporate Transport Since 2015

  Technology

Powered by Smart Transport Tech

We leverage cutting-edge technology to ensure safety, transparency and real-time visibility for every trip we operate.

GPS / IVMS Live Tracking

Real-time vehicle location, trip status and advanced vehicle location (AVL) services with health metric reporting.

Safetrax Commuter App

Employee app on iOS & Android — trip summary, self-boarding options, schedules, active trips and future trip visibility.

CCTV Live Monitoring

Installed CC cameras with live viewing to monitor driver behaviour and employee movement in larger capacity vehicles.

Panic Button & DMS

Physical panic alert device installed in every vehicle. Driver Monitoring System (DMS) detects harsh braking, lane swerving and aggressive driving.

RFID Attendance

RFID readers capture real-time employee attendance on boarding — eliminating manual records and ensuring accountability.

Face Detector

Face recognition for attendance and to avoid unauthorized entries in vehicles — deployed on client request and approval.

Real-Time Notifications

Employee notifications for driver reaching, ETA and ETD. Regular interaction through WhatsApp groups for seamless communication.

Safety Sensors

Smoke detectors and rear-view cameras installed in all vehicles to prevent fire hazards and eliminate blind-spot accidents.
